Signature Spirit

Hemp Craft Gin

A revolutionary spirit born in Goa. Our Hemp Craft Gin blends traditional juniper notes with the earthy, nutty undertones of legally sourced Himalayan hemp seeds. The result is an exceptionally smooth, botanical-forward gin that redefines the classic.

Tasting Notes

Crisp juniper, vibrant citrus peel, peppery spice, and a smooth, earthy hemp finish.

Perfect Serve

Premium Indian Tonic over ice, garnished with a slice of grapefruit and a sprig of rosemary.
