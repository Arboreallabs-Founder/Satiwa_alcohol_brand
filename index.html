The Happy High Gin

- A NEW KIND OF SPIRIT

Born in Goa.
Slow-Distilled.

Satiwa is a homegrown hemp craft spirits brand founded by Sahil Adwalpalkar. We pioneered India's hemp-infused gin category, crafting spirits that are slow-distilled under a tropical moon.

- THE COLLECTION

Three Spirits. One Philosophy.

Hemp Craft Gin

40% ABV, 750ml

The Original. The Happy High.

A London dry gin, Goan at heart, using hemp as a key botanical alongside juniper, orange, black pepper, and cinnamon. Light, fruity and floral on the nose with a distinctly nutty flavour.

Hemp Craft Rum

40% ABV, 750ml

Sugarcane. Smoke. Soul.

Crafted with notes of cardamom and rich sugarcane warmth. A spirit built for adventure and long, lazy nights.

Baby Pink Gin

40% ABV, 750ml

Soft. Sharp. Strawberry-Bright.

A sweeter, fruit-forward variant with a vibrant pink character for playful, spirited occasions.
