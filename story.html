Our Roots

The Satiwa Story

It started with a simple idea in the heart of Goa: to create a spirit that captures the relaxed, unhurried essence of tropical living, while introducing a completely unique botanical to the world of craft distillation.

Satiwa was born from a desire to do things differently. We spent years researching botanicals before we found our magic ingredient: legally sourced, premium Himalayan hemp seeds. When toasted and distilled, these seeds impart a rich, nutty, and exceptionally smooth character that traditional spirits lack.

Small Batch, Big Soul

Every drop of Satiwa is distilled in small copper pot stills in Goa. We believe in quality over quantity, taking the time to perfectly balance our botanicals. We source locally wherever possible, supporting Indian farmers and sustainable practices.

Whether you're sipping our classic Hemp Craft Gin by the beach or enjoying our dark Rum under the stars, Satiwa is an invitation to slow down, connect, and experience the "Happy High."
